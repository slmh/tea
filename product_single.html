Misty Morning Tea - Premium Loose Leaf Green Tea (4 oz) - High Caffeine - Classic & Malty

$17.95 Quantity Quantity
− +

Misty Morning is a robust long leaf style tea that features a good body with hints of smokiness. This tea will have malty notes similar to Yunnan black teas. But since Misty Morning is prepared as a green tea, it means it is a truly unique and wonderful experience that will cross lines and breach many tea drinker's ideas of a Classic Green Tea.

Try this tea at varying temperatures to see how it evolves as it cools off.

Brew for 2-3 minutes at 180° using 1-1.5 teaspoons (2g-3g) of the dry leaf.
